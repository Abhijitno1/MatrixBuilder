Mathematics of Nature

This site contains two links. First gives examples of Balanced Number matrices called Magic Squares used by ancient Indians in fortune telling and in worship of gods. Within constructed squares, the sum of numbers in any row, column or diagonal is exactly same. The second link gives example of a famous fractal known as Buddha Brot or Ganesha Brot. This fractal shape is formed using a single mathematical equation that resembles shape of Hindu god Ganesha and alternatively Buddha. Coincidently their birth stores are also linked.
